TikTok is a social media platform that focuses primarily on short form video content that can be consumed quickly, this makes it an addicting source of instant dopamine. These videos are also curated by an algorithm that always finds content that you are interested in. This makes it difficult to close the app and stop scrolling because you are never getting bored of what you are seeing. And before you know it, you are doom scrolling for hours. When users are addicted, this can be a huge waste of time and source of procrastination.
