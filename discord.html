Discord is a very popular app for communication among young people. Users can chat, voice call and video call with other users on this app. What sets Discord apart is its ability to host entire communities of people with a common interest through Discord servers. While this helps people connect, it can also paradoxically foster social isolation. When users spend too much time on Discord, it can replace face to face interactions. This can be especially detrimental when the user is young and still developing socially.
