Instagram is one of the most popular social media apps today. It helps friends stay connected with each other as well as connecting celebrities to fans. However, excessive use of Instagram can have numerous negative effects on the user's mental health. One of the problems most common among younger users of Instagram is the issue of unhealthy social comparisons. Seeing the idealized versions of people on Instagram can create unrealistic body standards and contribute to body dysmorphia. This feeling of inadequacy can lead to self-esteem and confidence issues
